Where It Struggles

Confusion tells the story

Glasses weakest class
The web crawl mixed eyeglasses and drinking glasses under the same label. The model had no way to separate them.
Tablet and phone confusion
Both are flat rectangles and look nearly identical at certain angles. Tablet finished at 70% precision, the lowest of the 10 classes.
Root cause
All 5 weak classes used web images only. The 5 webcam classes all had near-perfect precision and recall.
